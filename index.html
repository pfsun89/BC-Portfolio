Three of the Most Important Concepts I have Learned in BATECH 189

Preparing and Planning a Website

We all know that good practice and hands-on experience can help us improve our skills and become the best we can be at what we do, but when working on a long-term project, it may be a good idea to incorporate a planning phase into the process. Planning allows coders to develop a strategy and figure out things like outlines and goals. The purpose is to flesh out a vision, develop a roadmap, and recognizing or predicting possible obstacles.

Semantic HTML

Semantic HTML is all about the syntax. It's used for defining sections or chunks of content for web pages. This facilitates the maintenance and interpretation of the author's content. At a first glance, this may seem a bit boring, but it's important to understand the basics and fundamentals before getting fancy..

User Experience

The main idea of user experience is to think about using the web page from the target audience's perspective. Goals can include maximizing the positivity and meaningfulness of a user's visit to establish potential customer loyalty, and improving the users' engagement with the website. Both website functionality and presentation play important roles. Gaining popularity and getting visitors to want to use the website are two halves of a decent plan for businesses to improve their credibility and earn the trust of their clients.
